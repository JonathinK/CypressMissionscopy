Covid-19 Response

Covid-19 has affected all of us in different ways. In the beginning we couldn't do very much abroad or internally for that matter. As restrictions eased up we started to take a much needed look around locally to give aid where it was needed. Currently the restrictions have eased up to a point where we can continue to travel abroad. We understand how covid has impacted the communities that we see on a daily basis and we will continue to do our part in aiding who we can. Cypress would like to thank the communities for allowing us to help and any of the donators who helped us channel financial aid or physical aid.

Efforts were focused on our own local communities to see where we can extend ourselves to help. We've been very busy in Asbury Park, Nj and Neptune, Nj.

We follow all of the CDC guidelines to protect ourselves and others around us when possible.

We moved quickly to get chrome books to kids for online schooling. We saw there was a dire need to get them. It's imperative that the youth can continue education.

Bulgaria

Malomir & Yambol

Sept, 2020

Cypress Missions came ot the remote village of Malomir, Bulgaria. The first objective here was repairing the church, which will serve as a local center in one the most vulnerable regions in Europe. Much needed repairs were performed in at the kindergarten in the village, as well as donating school materials to more than 50 children in the village of Tenevo.
