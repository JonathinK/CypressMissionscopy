Bulgaria

Malomir & Yambol

Sept, 2020

Cypress Missions came ot the remote village of Malomir, Bulgaria. The first objective here was repairing the church, which will serve as a local center in one the most vulnerable regions in Europe. Much needed repairs were performed in at the kindergarten in the village, as well as donating school materials to more than 50 children in the village of Tenevo.
