One Goal

One Mission

Organizing humanitarian aid to help people in need around the world

Haiti

Haiti is one of the poorest countries in the world. With a large portion of the country living under the poverty line or even worse conditions. We are very excited to be a part of helping the community restore and grow.

Bahamas

Hurricane Dorian was one of the worst natural disasters in bahamas history. The bahamas were completely ravaged and needed help with rebuilding structures, donating cloths and food. Cypress missions will be helping rebuild the Abaco Islands.

Puerto Rico

In September of 2017 hurricane Maria absolutely ravaged Puerto Rico, leaving many areas looking like a warzone. We've had 9 total trips here bringing different aid each time. From water filters to generators, each trip has been successful.

Cypress Missions breaks the typical missionary mold

How We Work

Short Trips

We organize small trips to bring aid and supplies to impoverished areas or areas ravaged by natural disasters.

Make Connections

On our travels we establish connections with local leaders, build friendships with locals and look for future locations to work with, seeing where we can be of help next.

Our Solution

We believe that by managing our time and organizing the proper resources with a plan we can help rebuild communities.

Who We Look For

*This is strictly volunteer work

Cypress Missions looks for like minded, creative and passionate individuals who want to donate their time to make a difference. Since this is a volunteer position, we want those who travel with us to understand that we take this very seriously. Even though we do have fun and establish great friendships, we are limited on time when we travel.
