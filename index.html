Covid-19 Response

The covid-19 pandemic has affected all of us in very different ways. While we haven't been able to help abroad because of travel restrictions, we've been helping in many other ways during the pandemic. Cypress Missions has taken a much needed look at our own local communities to see where we can lend a hand to help. Labtops have been in dire need for online schooling and we have seen the struggle to get them so we took action to get chrome books out to kids for online learning. It is imperative that kids can continue their education. We are also completing local projects around our communities in Neptune, NJ and Asbury park, NJ. Covid has been rough for all over us but we will continue to do our part in helping where possible. Thank you for all your support. We will continue to update you all with more projects in the local communities and let you know when we are able to continue our partner ship projects.

How We Work

Freddie Organizes short trips with a team of volunteers to distribute supplies and rebuild buildings, homes and other structures in communities. By having a plan and structure it helps us to better achieve our goals on trips. Since we are non-profit all the money you donate goes to funding our purpose.

Who We Look For

Cypress missions looks for like minded individuals who want to help make a difference. This isn't a job. No one gets paid. We donate our time for others who can't or don't have the means to help themselves while making friends and memories along the way. If you feel like you want to make a difference reach out to us.

Haiti

Haiti is one of the poorest countries in the world. With a large portion of the country living under the poverty line or even worse conditions. We are very excited to be a part of helping the community restore and grow.

Bahamas

Hurricane Dorian was one of the worst natural disasters in bahamas history. The bahamas were completely ravaged and needed help with rebuilding structures, donating cloths and food. Cypress missions will be helping rebuild the Abaco Islands.

Puerto Rico

In September of 2017 hurricane Maria absolutely ravaged Puerto Rico, leaving many areas looking like a warzone. We've had 9 total trips here bringing different aid each time. From water filters to generators, each trip has been successful. Read more of our incredible travels.
