Covid-19 Response

The covid-19 pandemic has affected all of us in very different ways. While we haven't been able to help abroad because of travel restrictions, we've been helping in many other ways during the pandemic. Cypress Missions has taken a much needed look at our own local communities to see where we can lend a hand to help. Labtops have been in dire need for online schooling and we have seen the struggle to get them so we took action to get chrome books out to kids for online learning. It is imperative that kids can continue their education. We are also completing local projects around our communities in Neptune, NJ and Asbury park, NJ. Covid has been rough for all over us but we will continue to do our part in helping where possible. Thank you for all your support. We will continue to update you all with more projects in the local communities and let you know when we are able to continue our partner ship projects.

Bahamas

Green Turtle Cay

Oct. 2019

Cypress Missions made our first trip to GTC, Bahamas to start rebuilding after hurricane Dorian. The main goal was to establish relationships and familiarize for future trips to better serve the community.

Green Turtle Cay

Nov, 2019

On our second trip to GTC we rebuilt a business and started cleaning out for new work. This time around we brought supplies to help the surrounding areas and again looked to build better relationships with the community.

Green Turtle Cay

Feb, 2020

This was the third trip cypress missions would take to the bahamas and the work is far from over here. On the front end of the trip we rebuilt a home for an 84 year old woman to mark the start of getting those that lost their home back into them. We started to help the school, while visiting the Marsh Harbor Hatian Community living in tents.

Green Turtle Cay

Dec, 2020

On the 4th trip to Green Turtle Cay, the team finished several kitchens and many other building projects. This trip specifically had an emphasis on educational supplies for the local communities. For right now this was our last trip because of covid but we did look for more opportunities to help in the future. Cypress will return here soon.
